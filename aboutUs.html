About Cy Salon

At Cy Salon, we believe a haircut is more than just style – it’s confidence, personality, and tradition. Since opening our doors, we’ve combined classic barbering with modern trends, ensuring every client leaves looking sharp and feeling their best.

Our History

Established in 2010, Cy Salon began as a small neighborhood barbershop with a big dream: to bring back the artistry of grooming. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted spot for modern gentlemen, while keeping our roots in traditional barbering.
